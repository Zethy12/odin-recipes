Simple Baby Hake dish

Baby hake is a small, tender white fish with a mild flavor and soft texture. It's easy to cook and works well in light meals like pan-fried dishes, fish tacos, or baked with herbs and lemon.

Ingredients

  • 4 Baby Hake (Cleaned & Deboned)
  • 3 Small Eggs
  • 1 tsp fresh milk
  • 1 tsp dry parsley
  • 1 cup cake flour
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Oil

Steps to making baby hake

  1. Mix flour, black pepper and salt then set aside.
  2. Mix eggs and milk with parsley
  3. Heat oil. Coat the hake in the flour mixture then dip in the liquid one. Now Deep Fry.
