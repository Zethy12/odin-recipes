Mouth watering Beef Curry dish

Beef curry is a hearty, flavorful dish made with tender beef simmered in a rich, spiced gravy. Itâ€™s often enjoyed with rice or flatbread and is known for its warm, aromatic taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef of your choice cut up into curry pieces
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 onion chopped
  • sliced potatoes
  • Beef stock
  • Spices
  • Tomato paste
  • 3 grated tomatoes
  • aromat

Steps to Beef Curry

  1. Heat oil in a pot, once hot add sliced onion.
  2. When the onion is starting to turn brown add all the spices and the beef stock.
  3. Add tomato paste and grated tomatoes and mix well.
  4. Wait for a few minutes and then add the beef cubes and stir.
  5. Cover and cook on medium heat for about 10mins or when liquids dry a bit and oil surfaces, add 500ml boiling water and cook till softened, add more water as needed.
  6. 15mins before end of cooking time, add potatoes.
  7. Sprinkle the aromat for flavor.
  8. Cook till potatoes are done, once they are soft add soup.
  9. Pour soup into a bowl and add half a cup water and pour it inside the pot and mix well.
  10. Close the pot and wait for about 3 minutes , after your curry will be ready to be served, so switch off the stove.
